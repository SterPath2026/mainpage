Advanced Therapeutics and Vaccine Delivery Advisory

SterPath supports drug product development, delivery platform evaluation, and early CMC strategy, enabling efficient transition from discovery to development and beyond. Activities focus on aligning formulation, delivery technologies, and manufacturability with target product profiles from the outset, reducing development risk and supporting robust, scalable pathways toward clinical evaluation.

Core Capabilities

Advanced Modality Research & Development Support

Support for mRNA/LNP systems, nanoparticle-based delivery, VLPs, proteins, biologics, vaccines, and other complex modalities requiring integrated delivery, formulation, and CMC thinking.

Discovery-to-Development Roadmapping

Structured planning to move promising research toward development, including technical roadmaps, key milestones, decision points, data-gap identification, and risk-based prioritization.

Early CMC and Drug Product Strategy

Guidance on formulation, stability, process design, manufacturability, scalability, and control strategy considerations before development risks become costly.

Technology Assessment and Development Readiness

Independent review of platforms, product concepts, data packages, and development plans to identify strengths, risks, data gaps, and practical next steps supporting investment, partnering, funding, or internal decision-making.
